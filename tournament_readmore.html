Welcome to the Battle For Giostone Game Night!

Battle For Giostone is a true Web3 MOBA game where players can battle against each other in 5vs5 matches, earn rewards, and participate in Esports events with their custom-made Heroes! Based on their abilities, Heroes will have unique looks, stats, and traits that can be used on the battlefield. By using team tactics and strategy, creative battlers will be able to defeat the enemy team and emerge victorious by destroying the enemy base or meeting another victory condition.
